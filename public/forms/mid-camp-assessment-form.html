Impact Kidz Summer Camp
Mid-Camp Assessment and Decision Record
To be completed by the Camp Coordinator where any child is presenting as unable to engage safely or appropriately in camp, for any reason. This is not SEND-specific — it applies to any situation where a child's continued attendance requires formal consideration. Keep on the child's file.
Section A — Child and situation
What has happened, when did it occur, who was involved, and what makes this a concern that requires formal assessment now?
Section B — Assessment of current situation
Work through each consideration below. These are not tick-box decisions — they require your professional judgement as Camp Coordinator. Be specific and factual in your notes.
1. Is the child's continued attendance presenting a risk to their own safety or wellbeing?
Consider whether the child is distressed, at risk of harm, or in a state that means camp is not a suitable environment for them right now.
Notes:
2. Is the child's behaviour or situation presenting a risk to other participants or staff?
Consider physical safety, emotional impact on other children, and the capacity of staff to manage the situation while maintaining safe supervision of the wider group.
Notes:
3. Is the child able to meaningfully engage with the camp programme at this time?
Consider whether the child is in a position — emotionally, physically, or behaviourally — to participate in activities in a way that is beneficial for them.
Notes:
4. Are there factors outside the camp's control contributing to this situation?
For example: something that happened at home, a disclosed or undisclosed additional need, a conflict with another child, a medical episode. Understanding context helps reach a proportionate decision.
Notes:
Section C — Steps taken before this assessment
Record what has already been tried. A formal assessment should not be the first response to a concern — it follows a staged process. Document that process here.
Section D — Decision
Provide a clear written rationale, particularly if the child is being sent home or excluded. This is the record that will be referred to if the decision is questioned.
Section E — Communication with parent or carer
Section F — Sign off
This form should be completed and signed on the day of the assessment. It is kept on the child's file and may be referred to if the decision is questioned by the family, artsdepot, or a third party.
